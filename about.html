About Stayable

Built on fifteen years of getting it right.

A Newcastle property management business looking after homes, owners, and guests across the coast, lake, and Hunter Valley.

Our Story

Fifteen years in the making.

At Stayable, we bring 15+ years of property management experience and 8 years in short-term accommodation and Airbnb management to support both property owners and guests. Our expertise in short term accommodation rental, Airbnb managing, and local property management Newcastle helps us deliver consistent results.

We know how to run accommodation profitably, keep guests happy, and give owners peace of mind. That's why Stayable is now one of Newcastle's trusted operators, backed by 6,000+ reviews and a strong track record in professional accommodation services.

Our Team

People just like you.

Stayable is powered by a dedicated team, trusted contractors, and professional systems operating 7 days a week. Our experienced staff specialises in property management, short-term accommodation, and Airbnb management, ensuring every home is cared for with reliability and consistency.

Behind the scenes, we're parents, hosts, and community members just like you, who understand the importance of trust, care, and quality when managing homes across Newcastle.
