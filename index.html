FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

How does the free trial work?⌄
You get 30 days completely free — no credit card required. Your store is fully live and guests can place real orders during your trial. When your trial ends, simply choose a plan to keep your store active.
Does Hostie Hub take a cut of my sales?⌄
No commission, ever. Your flat yearly subscription ($99 or $199) is all you pay us. Every dollar your guests spend goes directly into your Stripe account — we never touch it. The only fees are Stripe's standard payment processing fees (typically around 1.7% + 30¢ per transaction).
Do I need a Stripe account to use Hostie Hub?⌄
Yes — Stripe is how your guests pay you directly. If you don't have one it only takes a few minutes to set up, and we walk you through it inside your dashboard.
What's my store address?⌄
When you sign up you choose a username (e.g. "sarah") and your store lives at hostiehub.com.au/store/sarah. Just drop that link into your Airbnb listing or welcome message.
Do guests need to create an account to order?⌄
No — guests just visit your store link and order directly. No login, no account, no friction. They enter their name, email, check-in dates and pay. That's it.
What kinds of products and services can I sell?⌄
Anything your guests might want — early check-in, late checkout, welcome gifts, breakfast baskets, experiences, airport transfers, pet fees, extra cleaning, flowers, champagne, tours. We give you 57 suggested products to start with across all categories.
How will I know what my guests ordered?⌄
Every order shows up in your host dashboard with the guest's name, check-in and check-out dates, property, what they ordered, and any notes they left you. You'll also receive an email notification for every new order.
How do I process a refund for a guest?⌄
Because payments go directly to your Stripe account, refunds are processed by you through your Stripe dashboard. Log in to Stripe, find the payment and issue a full or partial refund from there.
Can I use Hostie Hub across multiple properties?⌄
Yes — your store works across all your properties. The Host plan covers 1 property and the Pro plan covers up to 10. Need more? Contact us about Enterprise.
Can I cancel my subscription?⌄
Yes, anytime. We don't believe in lock-ins. If you cancel, your store stays active until the end of your current billing year. And if you come back, your store and products will still be there.
