Why is it free for hosts?

Because hosts already have enough costs. We make our money from local businesses paying to be discovered by your guests. The bigger and stronger the network of hosts, the better it is for everyone — partners reach more guests, hosts get more partner offerings to feature, and guests get a better stay.
