Hostie Hub lets you offer your guests amazing add-ons and extras — delivered straight to their door or ready on arrival. Here's how to get started:

1
Set up your store branding
Go to Store Settings in your dashboard and add your store name, colours and logo.
2
Add your properties
Go to Properties and add each property you manage. Guests will pick which one they're staying at.
3
Add products & services
Pick from our preset library — welcome hampers, experiences, late check-out — or create your own.
4
Connect Stripe to get paid
Link your Stripe account so payments go directly to your bank. Hostie Hub never touches your money.
5
Share your store link with guests
Drop your store link into your Airbnb welcome message. Guests browse and order directly.
🤝 Discover local partner offers
Check out Local Products in your dashboard — local businesses in your area have listed offers you can add to your store for free.
