How this works:

Hosts in your area add your listing to their guest store — a personalised page their guests open before and during their stay. Your listing sits alongside the host's own offerings (early check-in, hampers, etc.) so guests see your business before they even arrive. Captive, ready-to-spend audience, zero ad spend.

Three ways you can list:

🛒 Sell a product — wholesale price, hosts add their margin. Wine, hampers, flowers, baby kits, anything physical that gets delivered to or waiting at the property.

🛎️ Service request — guests submit a request through the host's store and you respond direct. Photographers, masseuses, private chefs, tour guides, anything booked-on-demand.

🔗 Booking link — guests click straight through to your existing booking system (Resy, OpenTable, your website, etc.). Restaurants, wineries, experiences, anything with its own booking flow. Optional voucher PDF can be auto-emailed when they click.
