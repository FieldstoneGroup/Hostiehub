Hostie Hub connects your business with guests staying at short-term rental properties in your area. Hosts add your listings to their guest stores — meaning guests discover you before they even arrive.

Here's how to get set up:

1
Create your first listing
Go to My Listings → Add Listing in your dashboard. Add your name, description and a photo. Listings with images get significantly more clicks.
2
Set your service area
Enter your suburb and click Apply — we'll cover all postcodes within 30km automatically. Only hosts in your area will see your listing.
3
Attach a voucher (optional)
Upload a voucher image or PDF — when a guest books, it's emailed to them automatically. Great for offers like complimentary drinks or discounts.
4
Publish and you're live
Click Publish and your offer is immediately available for local hosts. Track clicks, views and redemptions in Analytics.
💛 Your 3-month free trial is active
Everything is free for the first 3 months. After that, $49/listing/month — only active listings count, and you can pause or deactivate any time.
