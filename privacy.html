Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: April 2025 · Fieldstone Group Pty Ltd ABN 75 656 604 376

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. We'll never sell your data or share it without your consent.

1. Who we are

Hostie Hub is operated by Fieldstone Group Pty Ltd (ABN 75 656 604 376), based in Newcastle, New South Wales, Australia. We provide a SaaS platform that allows short-term rental hosts to create a branded extras store for their guests.

For any privacy matters, contact us at [email protected].

2. Information we collect

From hosts (account holders)

  • Name, email address and password (for account creation and login)
  • Business name, store name and branding preferences
  • Property details (nicknames and addresses)
  • Postcode (used to match local partner vouchers)
  • Stripe Connect account details (handled securely by Stripe)
  • Subscription and billing information (processed by Stripe)

From guests (store visitors)

  • Name, email address, check-in and check-out dates
  • Order details and any notes provided at checkout
  • Payment information (processed directly by Stripe — we never see or store card details)

Automatically collected

  • Browser type, device type and IP address
  • Pages visited and time spent on site (via analytics cookies)

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Hostie Hub account
  • Process guest orders and send order confirmation emails
  • Send hosts notifications about new orders
  • Send subscription and trial reminder emails
  • Improve and maintain the platform
  • Respond to support enquiries
  • Meet our legal and compliance obligations

4. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with trusted third-party services required to operate the platform:

  • Stripe — payment processing and host payouts. Stripe processes all payment card data directly and is PCI-DSS compliant. See Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (order confirmations, notifications). See resend.com for their privacy policy.
  • Supabase — secure database hosting for account and order data.
  • Cloudflare — website infrastructure and security.

Partner businesses and vouchers

If a guest purchases a voucher from a local partner business through the Hostie Hub store, that partner business will receive the guest's name and email address for the purpose of fulfilling the voucher. Guests are informed of this at the point of purchase.

We do not share data with any other third parties without your explicit consent.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to make Hostie Hub work. There are two types:

Essential cookies (always on)

These are required for the platform to function — they manage your login session and keep your account secure. You cannot opt out of these cookies as they are necessary for the service to work.

Analytics cookies (optional)

We use analytics cookies to understand how people use our site so we can improve it. You can opt out of analytics cookies at any time by adjusting your browser settings or using our cookie preference centre (available in the footer of this site).

6. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription and close your account, your data is deleted within 90 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance purposes.

Order records may be retained for up to 7 years in accordance with Australian taxation law.

7. Your rights

Under Australian privacy law and the Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored on secure, encrypted servers. Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe and is never stored on our servers.

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at [email protected].

9. International users

Hostie Hub is operated from New South Wales, Australia, and this Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of New South Wales and the Commonwealth of Australia. By using Hostie Hub from outside Australia, you consent to the collection and processing of your data in accordance with Australian privacy law and this policy.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the date at the top of this page and notify active users by email if the changes are material. Continued use of Hostie Hub after changes are published constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
